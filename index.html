About Our Hotel

The History Of Our Hotel

The peaceful resort of St George South and the quiet surroundings at the Alexander Beach Hotel make for a relaxing holiday, with rooms on self-catering basis.
The hotel was built by the current owners father and is a family run business. The Alexander Beach Hotel offers simple yet comfortable accommodation, with on-site parking available.

Great locations

Within 200 metres of the hotel you can find amazing beaches, go further afield and there are olive groves and little coffee shops to enjoy

Fantastic meals

The resort of St. Georges is small but has a plethora of restaraunts to enjoy, all family businesses and everyone has their favourites, go and find yours!

Hotel Facilities

The hotel has air conditioning in every room, a fridge and some catering facilities depending on room type. Breakfast is served at the pool bar and food is available all day.
